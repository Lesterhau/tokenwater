AI data centers consumed billions of liters of water last year. Microsoft: +34%. Google: +20%. No one tells you your share. TokenWater generates a memory snippet you paste into your AI tools once — after that, your AI tracks your usage automatically.

🔒 Anonymous Impact Reporting

TokenWater counts your installation in a public community total — showing the world how many people are choosing to hold AI infrastructure accountable. We don't know who you are. No name, no email, no IP address, no location. Just an 8-character alphanumeric code embedded in your snippet that counts you once, across all your devices. To opt out, see Advanced settings below.

The Water Math
The Proxy Metric
~100ml of water consumed per 1,000 tokens processed. Derived from data center PUE data and cooling water intensity ratios. Directional estimate — actual consumption varies by provider, region, and model.
